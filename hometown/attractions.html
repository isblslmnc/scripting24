There are multiple island-hopping packages that are famous at El Nido which will take you to different beaches and lagoons. Tourists usually go kayaking at the lagoons that are included in the bundle and snorkeling for some parts of the islands. You will see the beautiful mangroves and coral reefs that El Nido is known for when snorkeling.

Besides the natural beauty of this place, I also noticed that their nightlife was extremely lively. After a relaxing day at the beach in the morning, you can go to their bars and clubs that provide drinks, tasty food and even live music in selected places.
