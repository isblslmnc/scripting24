El Nido's population, which was just about 50,494 in 2020, is quite small. It is mostly made up of indigenous people which, allows visitors to encounter a variety of cultures. The locals are very warm and welcoming people. It was easy to navigate around the place because they were always willing to help newcomers.

I also noticed that they value and preserve the place so much, which is how El Nido stays as enchanting as it is throughout the years.
