Isabel Salamanca is a first-year student in the Multimedia Program focused on Graphic Design & Digital Video at Lethbridge Polytechnic. She took an interest in drawing and painting as a kid which led to her interest in designing some of her own tattoos and to learn more about Graphic Design in college. She also likes taking videos and photos for memories which she will edit just for fun.

Isabel was born and raised in the Philippines. She then moved to United Arab Emirates back in 2016 to continue her high school education. She completed her junior high school education in 2019 and then moved back to the Philippines in 2021 to finish her senior high school and start her college. In 2023, she decided to start processing her papers to move to Canada to continue her college education. She adapts to new environments with ease and does not get easily panicked when things are not going her own way.

Interests

MUSIC

Isabel can't live a day without listening to music. I grew up listening to different kinds of genres and as I get older, I also started realizing that I am fond of live music. I have watched at least one concert since 2022 and it just kind of became a goal to see live music every year. It's the one thing that I'll always look forward to.

She also started being a fan of One Direction back in 2010 and wasn't able to see them live as a group when she was a kid. So she never misses a solo concert of the members now.

TRAVEL

Isabel loves traveling and exploring new places. Getting to know new cultures and seeing other countries' landscapes and skyscrapers is an experience that she always looks forward to. She likes riding the plane because she loves seeing beautiful views from above and she loves seeing the city lights.

FORMULA 1

Getting interested in Formula 1 was something that was very unexpected that happened last 2022, She just stumbled upon a series about it and decided to play it in the background while she was doing something else. However, that show got her hooked into it that she decided to start watching the actual race every Sunday. There are 10 racing team and 20 drivers of the grid. Isabel's favourite team is Scuderia Ferrari and one of their driver, Carlos Sainz Jr.
